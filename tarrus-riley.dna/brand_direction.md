# Riley Footwear — Brand Direction
**Artist:** Tarrus Riley
**Product:** Leather shoes & slippers
**Brand Name:** Riley Footwear
**Short Name:** Riley

---

## Concept

Warm, unhurried, sovereign craft. This is not European luxury imitated — it is Jamaican artisan with its own authority. Tarrus Riley's sound is mature, soulful, and built to last. The footwear brand carries the same weight: things made properly, for people who know the difference.

The product line focuses on leather dress shoes, loafers, and slippers. Understated silhouettes, quality materials, nothing loud. The shoe speaks before the wearer does.

---

## Logo

- **"Riley"** in a flowing italic script — large, dominant, the whole mark
- **"Tarrus"** in small spaced caps sitting above it, almost secondary — like a first name on a surname that already carries the room
- Warm gold or cognac as the wordmark colour on dark backgrounds; deep brown on cream/light backgrounds
- No icon needed — the script is the identity

---

## Typography

- **Wordmark:** Flowing italic serif (Cormorant Garamond Italic or similar) — "Riley" in cursive
- **Supporting text:** Small spaced caps, clean serif — Playfair Display or EB Garamond
- **Body / labels:** Light weight sans — Space Grotesk or similar, very small, lots of breathing room

## AI Image Direction

**Hero shot:** A single leather loafer on a warm mahogany surface, side-lit with soft diffused natural light. Slight shadow. No background clutter. Shot feels like it belongs in a small-run lookbook, not a catalogue.

**Texture shot:** Extreme close-up of hand-stitched leather seam. Cognac leather, gold thread. Almost abstract.

**Lifestyle shot:** The slipper on a wooden floor beside a record player. No model visible — just the shoe, the wood, the warm light.

**Packaging shot:** Matte cream box with "Riley" embossed in gold on the lid. Half-open, tissue paper folded back revealing the shoe inside.

**Lighting rule:** Always warm. No cold white light. No flash. Feels like late afternoon sun through a window.
